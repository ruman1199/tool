How Our Face Age Guesser Works

Our advanced AI technology analyzes facial features to estimate a person's age. The algorithm examines various facial characteristics including skin texture, wrinkles, facial structure, and other age-related markers to provide an accurate age prediction.

Tips for Best Results

For the most accurate age estimation:

  • Use a clear, well-lit frontal face photo
  • Ensure the face is clearly visible and not obscured
  • Avoid heavily filtered or edited images
  • Use images with neutral facial expressions
